What is VisionMart?

VisionMart is a modern E-Commerce Order & Inventory Management System designed to simplify business operations. It enables administrators to manage products, categories, inventory, and sales efficiently.

Customers can browse products, add items to cart, and complete purchases securely. The platform combines powerful dashboard controls with real-time tracking and secure authentication.

System Features

Order Management

Customers can add products to cart and checkout easily.

Inventory Tracking

Monitor stock levels and low-stock alerts in real time.

Product Management

Add, update, and manage product listings.

Category Controls

Organize products efficiently.

Reports & Analytics

View sales and inventory reports instantly.

Secure Access

JWT-based authentication system.
